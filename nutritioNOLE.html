Why NutritioNOLE?

Healthy never been so easy! There are two types of people the fit, and the fit wannabes. It turns out we found ourselves in the wannabe because as much as we like to exercise, we also like to eat. So why not make it easier and more fun? We found there was no app or tool that could help up address issue. So our main focus is to make a tool to help students become healthier, one step at a time and all in one place.
